WHAT IS KOMBUCHA?

If you're unfamiliar with kombucha, it is a fermented tea with probiotic cultures (gut loving biome), enzymes and trace nutrients used mainly for digestion and gut health.

It's a functional health drink which is vegan, raw, low in sugar and made with 100% natural ingredients.

WHY CHOOSE TWISTED KOMBUCHA?

Twisted Kombucha was developed during my Masters in Food Science, carrying out microbiological, biochemical and analytical testing.

After critically analysing kombucha in the laboratory I discovered the functional health benefits of the magical microbes created during fermentation.

This validated the physiological roles kombucha plays to aid in healthy digestive and immune function.

HOW IT'S MADE?

Kombucha is an all-natural traditionally fermented beverage, produced in the authentic old fashioned way. It starts with a pot of sweet tea brewed with loose leaves and a SCOBY. It's left to spontaneously ferment for a few weeks resulting in a tangy, sweet beverage which delights your senses, and is filled with living nutrition that invigorates your body.

We do not adulterate our kombucha in any way. That defeats the purpose of drinking kombucha for 'live probiotic' benefits.
